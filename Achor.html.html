Description

MEOW Is Your Name is a fast paced and highly entertaining
game that is sure to captivate even the most jaded teenagers.
For native English speakers, this game can be used for team building, a class reward, or a camp activity.
For ESL students it teaches the question words who, what, when, where, why, how and which.
Students will first recognize and then use the question words in a variety of situations.
